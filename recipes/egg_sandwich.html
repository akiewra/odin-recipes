Egg Sandwich

Egg Sandwiches are my favorite breakfast meal to make when I'm hungry. The best part is they're simple and easy to make to your liking.

I will be explaining how to make a turkey egg sandwich, but feel free to choose different toppings of your choice - or none at all!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Everything Bagel
  • Turkey Deli Meat
  • Ketchup

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Eggs: I usually fry my eggs on stovetop and flip once after 3 minutes.
  2. As your eggs are cooking, prepare the bagel. I toast mine for 4 minutes so I get a light crisp.
  3. Once the eggs form solid, toss a slice of cheese and turkey ontop and cover the pan.
  4. Once the cheese is melted to your desire, remove the eggs and slide them onto the bagel!
  5. Optional: Add whatever seasonings/dressings you want. I prefer ketchup and lightly peppered.

Enjoy!
